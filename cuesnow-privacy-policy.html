4. Location Services - Detailed Explanation

Since CuesNow's core feature involves location-based reminders, here's exactly how we handle location:

4.1 How Location Works

  • Geofencing: We use iOS geofencing to detect when you enter saved places
  • On-Device Processing: All location processing happens on your iPhone via Apple's Core Location
  • No Server Communication: Your location is NEVER sent to our servers or any third party
  • No Location History: We do not store where you have been

4.2 Location Permission Options

  • While Using: Location only accessed when app is open
  • Always: Required for arrival notifications when app is closed (recommended)
  • Never: App works without location, but arrival reminders are disabled

4.3 Your Control

  • Disable location access anytime in iOS Settings
  • Delete saved places to remove geofences
  • Use the app without location for time-based reminders only

5. Voice Input (Pro Feature)
