Games not loading properly?

1. Force close the app and restart it
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
4. If the issue persists, contact us at the email above

How does the daily challenge system work?

Each game uses a unique "seed" that changes daily. This means everyone gets the same puzzles on the same day, making it fair and competitive!

Can I play offline?

Yes! All games work completely offline. No internet connection required.

How do I change the language?

PicoBrain automatically uses your device's language setting. Currently supports English and Korean.

How can I turn off ads?

Ads appear after completing games to support the app's development. We've designed them to be non-intrusive and appear only after you've finished playing.
