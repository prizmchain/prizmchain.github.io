Focused - Study Planner & Exam Tracker

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

Privacy First - Our Commitment

Focused is designed as a local-first study planner for exam preparation, focus sessions, and wrong-answer review.

  • No account sign-up is required
  • No advertising SDKs are used
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are used
  • Your study plans, subjects, sessions, wrong-answer items, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • Camera, Photo Library, Microphone, and Speech Recognition access are used only for features you choose to use
  • Purchase status is processed by Apple and RevenueCat so Focused Pro can be unlocked and restored

About Study Data, Photos, and Recordings

Focused may store study-related content locally on your device.

  • Focused does not upload your study plans, wrong-answer images, recordings, or notes to our servers
  • Wrong-answer images and selected study images are stored locally when you add them
  • Voice recordings and transcripts are stored locally when you use recording features
  • You can revoke app permissions at any time in iOS Settings

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Device Storage

Focused stores your study data locally on your device. This may include:

  • Study goals and exam D-Day data
  • Subjects, tasks, and daily plans
  • Focus sessions and study history
  • Study statistics and progress summaries
  • Wrong-answer review items and attached images
  • Voice recordings and transcripts created inside the app
  • Theme, notification, widget, and app settings
  • Pro access state cached for app functionality

3.2 App Group Storage for Widgets

Focused may use local App Group storage to share limited study summary data with its widgets and Live Activity components. This may include D-Day, today's study time, next task, streak, and progress display data.

3.3 Photo Library Access

Focused uses Photo Library access only when needed for features such as selecting wrong-answer images or saving share cards. Images are not uploaded to our servers.

3.4 Camera, Microphone, and Speech Recognition

Focused may use the Camera, Microphone, and Speech Recognition permissions when you choose to capture study images, record audio, or convert speech to text. Recordings and transcripts are stored locally unless you delete the app or remove them.

3.5 Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app
  • Deleting the app may remove locally stored Focused data from your device
  • Purchase records are handled by Apple and RevenueCat so purchases can be restored

5. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Study goals and exams: To generate D-Day plans, daily study plans, and progress estimates
  • Subjects and tasks: To organize study priorities and daily work
  • Focus sessions: To record study time and update statistics
  • Wrong-answer items: To schedule review reminders and track mistake recovery
  • Photos and images: To attach images to review notes or save share cards when you choose
  • Recordings and transcripts: To help create study notes from speech when you choose
  • Widget and Live Activity data: To show quick progress and timer status on supported system surfaces
  • Purchase information: To unlock or restore Focused Pro

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

  • Local-first design: Study data is stored on your device
  • iOS sandboxing: App data is protected by iOS app sandboxing
  • Secure purchases: Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • No ad tracking: Focused does not integrate advertising trackers

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, but Focused is designed to minimize unnecessary data exposure.

8. Children's Privacy

Focused is intended as a study and productivity utility. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  • No account registration is required
  • No social features are provided
  • No public user profiles are created
  • No user-to-user messaging is provided

9. Your Rights and Choices

9.1 Access and Control

  • View Data: Your study plans, subjects, tasks, sessions, statistics, and wrong-answer items are visible inside the app

9.2 Data Deletion
