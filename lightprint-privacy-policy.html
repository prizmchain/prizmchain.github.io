Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Lightprint keeps your light recipes on your device. We do not run our own server, require an account, or upload your saved recipes, thumbnails, camera notes, exposure values, calibration settings, or exported cards. In-app purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Lightprint ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information. Lightprint is an iPhone light meter, exposure recipe, and scene matching tool for photographers and video creators.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials
  • Your saved light recipes, thumbnails, notes, camera/lens/profile details, or calibration values on our servers
  • Precise GPS location data
  • Contacts, microphone recordings, browsing history, advertising identifiers, or device fingerprints
  • Analytics, behavioral tracking, advertising network data, or crash reporting data

2.3 Data Processed by Third-Party Services

We do not use advertising networks, third-party analytics, behavioral tracking SDKs, or third-party crash reporting tools.

3. How We Use Information

  • Light measurement: Camera data is used on-device to estimate exposure and white balance.
  • Recipe storage: Saved recipes and thumbnails remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them.
  • Exports: PNG cards and CSV files are generated locally and shared only through iOS tools you choose to use.
  • Purchases: Apple processes payment, and RevenueCat verifies whether your Pro entitlement is active.

4. Permissions We Request

PermissionPurposeRequired
CameraEstimate exposure, white balance, Kelvin/tint, and capture local recipe thumbnailsRequired for core metering

Lightprint does not request microphone, contacts, precise location, advertising tracking, or push notification permissions. If you export or share files, iOS may present system share or file dialogs controlled by Apple.

5. In-App Purchases

Lightprint Pro is available as a one-time lifetime purchase:

  • Purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat
  • We do not receive your payment card information or Apple ID password
  • RevenueCat helps verify and restore your Pro entitlement
  • You can restore purchases using the same Apple ID, subject to Apple's App Store rules
  • This is a one-time purchase, not a subscription

6. Data Retention

  • Local recipes and presets: Stored until you delete them or uninstall the App
  • Preferences and calibration: Stored locally until reset or uninstall
  • Purchase status: Stored locally and can be restored through Apple/RevenueCat
  • Exported files: Controlled by you after export or sharing

Uninstalling the App removes locally stored app data from your device. Purchase records may remain with Apple and RevenueCat as needed for purchase restoration, fraud prevention, tax, accounting, and legal obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

Lightprint does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not include social features, public profiles, chat, advertising, or user-generated content sharing through our servers.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete recipes and presets inside the App
  • Uninstall the App to remove locally stored app data
  • Control camera permission in iOS Settings
  • Contact us with privacy questions
  • Refer to Apple and RevenueCat for data they process as independent third-party services

9. International Users

Lightprint may be available worldwide. Core app data is processed locally on your device. Purchase processing may involve Apple and RevenueCat systems in their respective regions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the revised policy at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
