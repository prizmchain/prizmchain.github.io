Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

DaySlip is a 100% local app. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your activity entries to any server. We do not require an account, email, or any personal information. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how DaySlip ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information. DaySlip is a daily activity receipt generator that operates entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

DaySlip does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Your daily activity entries or receipt content
  • Photos, contacts, or files from your device
  • GPS location or precise location data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers or device fingerprints
  • Analytics or usage tracking data

2.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to any server:

Data Purpose Storage
Premium Purchase Status Remember if you purchased PRO Device only
Theme Preference Remember your selected theme Device only
Activity Entries Current session receipt items Device only (session)

2.3 Data Processed by Third-Party Services

When you make an in-app purchase, the following third-party services are involved:

Service Purpose Data Involved
Apple App Store Payment processing Handled entirely by Apple — we never receive your payment info
RevenueCat In-app purchase management Anonymous device identifier, purchase status

We do NOT use any advertising networks, behavioral tracking, analytics services, or crash reporting tools.

3. How We Use Information

Since DaySlip operates locally, there is minimal data processing:

  • Local preferences: Your theme and purchase status are stored on-device for app functionality
  • In-app purchases: RevenueCat processes purchase verification using an anonymous identifier
  • Image export: When you tap "PRINT RECEIPT", the receipt is rendered as an image locally on your device — no server involved
  • Sharing: When you share a receipt image, it goes through the native iOS share sheet — we have no involvement in or visibility into this process

4. Permissions We Request

Permission Purpose Required
None DaySlip does not request any special permissions N/A

DaySlip does NOT request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos library, or push notifications.

5. In-App Purchases

DaySlip PRO is available as a one-time lifetime purchase:

  • Purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat
  • We do NOT receive your payment information or Apple ID
  • RevenueCat receives only an anonymous device identifier and purchase status
  • You can restore purchases on any device using the same Apple ID
  • This is a one-time purchase — no subscriptions, no recurring charges

6. Data Retention

  • Activity entries: Session-based, cleared when you tap "Clear All" or close the app
  • Preferences: Stored locally until you uninstall the app
  • Purchase status: Stored locally; can be restored via Apple ID after reinstall
  • Exported images: Saved to your device or shared via the share sheet — we have no access

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. Your in-app purchase can be restored via your Apple ID.

7. Children's Privacy

DaySlip is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. We do not collect any personal information from any user, including children. The app does not contain user-generated content sharing, social features, or any mechanism that could expose personal information.

8. Your Rights

8.1 Data Control

  • All your data is on your device — you have full control
  • Delete activity entries at any time using "Clear All"
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data
  • No server-side data to request deletion of (we don't have any)

8.2 EU Users (GDPR)

Since DaySlip does not collect or process personal data on any server, GDPR obligations are minimal. However, if you have questions about RevenueCat's data processing, please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

9. International Users

DaySlip is available worldwide in 7 languages (English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, German, French, Spanish). Since all data is processed locally on your device, no cross-border data transfer occurs for core app functionality. In-app purchase processing may involve Apple and RevenueCat servers in their respective regions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Posting the new policy at this URL

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
